Decompress your L2-L3, open tight hips, prep shoulders. Do this before any training day.
⚠️ Back Safety
  • Work to the edge of comfort — never into pain
  • Back twinges = reduce range, slow down
  • Controlled movement only — no bouncing
Day 1 — Lower Body
⚠️ Lower Back Protocol
  • Neutral spine on ALL exercises — no rounding under load
  • Stop if lower back twinges — form before weight
  • Leave 2–3 reps in the tank every set
💪 Warm-Up Set
On your first exercise, do 1 warm-up set at ~50–60% of working weight for 8–10 easy reps. Gets joints ready, protects your blood pressure, locks in form before going heavy.
Day 2 — Upper Push & Pull
💪 Warm-Up Set
Bench Press: do 1 warm-up set at ~95 lbs for 8 easy reps before your working sets. Gets your shoulders and pecs fully warmed up.
Day 3 — KB HIIT Circuit
Circuit Format
